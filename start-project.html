Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website can take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications may take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after reviewing your requirements.

What does a security audit include?

+

Our security audits include comprehensive vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, security posture evaluation, detailed recommendations report, and a consultant call to discuss findings.

Do you sign NDA?

+

Yes, absolutely. We take confidentiality seriously and are happy to sign NDAs before discussing your project details. Your ideas and data are always protected.

What do you need from us to start?

+

To get started, we need a clear understanding of your project goals, any existing documentation or designs, access credentials (if applicable), and your preferred timeline. We'll guide you through the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, UPI, and major credit cards. Payment terms are typically structured as milestones, with an initial deposit to begin work.
